About US

Who We Are

Lux Events Planning was established in 2021 and is a professional events management events planning and events services company based in Gauteng. Our motto is to curate exclusive events for priceless memories for our clients through our dedication and creativity We Specialize in events as a whole, any event we are there! The Lux Events planning team focuses on attention to detail to ensure that every occasion is an unforgettable experience with remarkable memories.

The Founder

Melissa Malotana, who is only 21 is the founder of the company Lux Events, She grew up as a creative child and always loved events and playing around with colors and decorative items, when Covid-19 hit in 2020 people started hosting intimate events and she volunteered and officially started her business
